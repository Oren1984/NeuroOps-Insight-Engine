Product Notes

The BI platform is designed to combine:
1. structured analytics
2. retrieval-augmented guidance
3. LLM-based business summaries
4. operational automation handoff to n8n

RAG usage:
- retrieve business notes
- combine them with current KPIs
- answer business questions with more context

n8n usage:
- simulate outgoing automation
- deliver executive summaries or alert payloads
- later connect to Slack, email, CRM, or ticketing systems

Executive Summary expectations:
- short
- action oriented
- written for product, support, and operations stakeholders
- based on current data rather than only static rules
